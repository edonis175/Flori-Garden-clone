Garden and landscaping from Haus FLORI

Discover with us how passion for nature and expertise go hand in hand.As a trusted partner with many years of experience in garden design and professional landscape design, we are committed to creating green living spaces that are in perfect harmony with their surroundings. Our family business brings not only expertise but also passion and dedication to every project.We design gardens that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also promote well-being and offer a sense of nature.

Where green dreams take root and blossom

Our history at FLORI is deeply rooted in our dedication to making your green visions a reality. We offer a comprehensive range of services covering every facet of garden design and landscaping. Whether it's the artistic installation of paving and natural stone, professional excavation and drainage work, the careful construction of fences and walls, or the expert planting of trees, shrubs, and perennials – our team will implement your project with precision and creativity. We are also your experienced partner for compensatory planting, ensuring your green paradise is sustainable.

Your green dreams, our sustainable mission

Our mission is to create spaces of relaxation and beauty that simultaneously respect the environment and promote sustainability.Our approach to garden design and landscape art combines quality, creativity, and commitmentto turn your green visions into reality.Welcome to a world where every garden is a work of art and a commitment to harmony with nature.

Our team is undoubtedly our greatest strength.
Each individual team member brings a wealth of knowledge and experience, as well as a passionate work ethic, to our company. In a highly respectful, collaborative, and inspiring working environment, we foster the individual growth of each team member, thus ensuring the overall success of FLORI GARTEN.
In all our business relationships, whether with customers, suppliers, or employees, we place particular emphasis on honesty, fairness, and integrity. Our top priority is to always ensure ethical and transparent business practices in order to sustainably strengthen the trust of our partners and customers. Our goal is to build strong and long-lasting partnerships based on trust and integrity.
Every customer, every request, and every project is unique to us. We take the time necessary to understand and respond to individual wishes and needs. Our ultimate goal is to not only meet expectations but to exceed them. Satisfied customers are the heart of our business.
Your family's safety is our top priority at FLORI GARTEN. We recognize that a pool is not just a place for relaxation and enjoyment, but also a responsibility. Therefore, we take great care to ensure that every pool we design and build meets the highest safety standards.
At FLORI GARTEN, we are committed to protecting the environment and the careful use of natural resources. By using sustainable construction practices and promoting environmentally friendly innovations, we actively contribute to resource conservation. Our goal is to make a positive contribution to the environment and shape a greener future through responsible action in every project.
The world of garden and pool construction is constantly evolving. Technological innovations, new materials, and designs are omnipresent. We stay at the cutting edge through continuous training and the use of innovative technologies. This ensures that our customers always benefit from the most modern and efficient solutions.
